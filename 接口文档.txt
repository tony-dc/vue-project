热门电影api接口,params为城市id参数
http://m.maoyan.com/ajax/movieOnInfoList?cityId='+params

影院api接口：params为城市id参数
http://m.maoyan.com/ajax/cinemaList?ci='+params

城市列表api接口
http://m.maoyan.com/dianying/cities.json

即将上映api接口,cityId,token,limit为需传入参数
http://m.maoyan.com/ajax/comingList?`ci=${cityId}&token=&limit=10`

电影详情页api接口:movieId为电影对应的id值参数
"http://m.maoyan.com/ajax/detailmovie?movieId="+movieId
